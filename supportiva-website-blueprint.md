## DESIGN OBSERVATIONS & UPGRADE RECOMMENDATIONS

### Current Site Weaknesses (for upgrade context)
1. **Built on Wix** — template-based, limited customization
2. **Reuses the same hero image** on 3 different pages (Solutions, Vision, Blog)
3. **Vision page is very thin** — only 2 short sections of content
4. **No team section** — no photos or bios of team members
5. **No testimonials or case study details** — projects page lists work but no client quotes
6. **No contact form visible** on main pages (only a "Get Started" link)
7. **Stats could be more impactful** — currently plain text numbers
8. **Blog has no dates, authors, or categories** visible on the listing page
9. **No search functionality** on the blog
10. **Copyright still says 2024**
11. **Mobile responsiveness** is Wix-default, not optimized
12. **No animations or micro-interactions** — very static feel
